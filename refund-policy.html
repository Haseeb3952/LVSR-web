Refund Policy for Link Vault Social Repository (LVSR)

Introduction

At Link Vault Social Repository (LVSR), we strive to ensure your satisfaction with our services. This Refund Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures related to refund requests for our premium features or paid versions. By using our paid services, you acknowledge and agree to this Refund Policy.

Eligibility for Refund

1. Refunds are only applicable to users who have subscribed to our premium features or paid versions.
2. To be eligible for a refund, you must submit a refund request within 3 days from the date of purchase.
3. Refunds will be issued only for the initial subscription fee paid and not for any subsequent renewals.

Grounds for Refund

You may request a refund under the following circumstances:

1. Technical Issues: If you experience significant technical difficulties with our services that prevent you from accessing or using the paid features, despite reasonable attempts to resolve the issues with our support team.
2. Unsatisfactory Service: If you find our premium features or paid versions do not meet your expectations or are materially different from what was advertised.
3. Unauthorized Purchase: If there is evidence of an unauthorized or fraudulent transaction related to your account.

Non-Eligibility for Refund

Refunds will not be provided in the following situations:

1. Failure to Cancel Subscription: If you fail to cancel your subscription before the renewal date and are charged accordingly.
2. Change of Mind: If you change your mind about using our paid services after the refund eligibility period has expired.
3. Violation of Terms: If your refund request is due to a violation of our Terms and Conditions or any other policies.
4. Other Services or Products: This Refund Policy only applies to LVSR's premium features or paid versions and does not cover any other services or products.

Refund Process

1. To request a refund, you must contact our support team at info@lvsr.io and provide the necessary details, including your purchase information and reason for the refund.
2. Our support team will review your request and may seek additional information to assess your eligibility.
3. If your refund request is approved, we will process the refund to the original payment method used for the purchase within 7 days.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Refund Policy, please contact our support team at info@lvsr.io. We are here to assist you.
